Ruoyan Kong is a second year master student majoring in Insurance and Big Data Finance and is expected to graduate in June, 2018 from the department of finance, Tsinghua University. Ruoyan Kong's research interests includes the adaptation and application of machine learning and game theory in E-commerce, finance, public policy, economics and mathematical models in enterprise risk management.
